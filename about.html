Our Story

A decade of innovation, dedicated to the future of farming.

Since 2016

Revolutionizing the Soil

EcoHarvest was founded with a single mission: to create machinery that respects the earth while empowering the farmer. We believe the future of agriculture isn't just about output, it's about balance.

From our humble beginnings in a small workshop to becoming the global leader in sustainable agri-tech, our focus remains on engineering machines that reduce carbon footprints without compromising power.

The Journey

Milestones that define our commitment.

2016

EcoHarvest Idea Born

Company founded by agricultural engineers aiming to replace diesel tractors with electric prototypes.

2019

Sovereign-E Launch

The first fully electric, high-performance tractor hits the market, winning the Global Innovation Award.

2023

Global Expansion

EcoHarvest fleet reaches 15+ countries, saving more than 500,000 tons of CO2 emissions worldwide.

2026

The AI Integration

Our fleet becomes fully integrated with AI-Soil-Sense, optimizing crop yields and water usage like never before.
